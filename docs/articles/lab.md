# Don't just Build a Portfolio, Foster Lab Culture

If you - like me - often find yourself experimenting with a product idea that relates to your company but it's not on the official road map, what can you do to get buy-in to work on it officially?

The dangers of working on any side project without authorization is that it is a distration from your primary task. As long as your main tasks are being completed on time, there should be room and down time for experimentation. With proper plan, experimentation could help you get noticed and even promoted.
